Panko Chicken

  • 600g Chicken breast
  • 1Â½ dl Wheat
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3dl Panko breadcrumbs
  • 2dl Frying oil
  1. Flatten the chicken to about 1/2 cm pieces.
  2. Crack the eggs in a deep plate and mix lightly.
  3. Pour the wheat and panko into two more deep plates.
  4. Dip the chicken in the wheat on both sides, then the eggs and then in the panko.
  5. Fry in a pan with the frying oil on 3 minutes on each side
