Carbonara

  • 300g Spaghetti
  • 140g Bacon
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 dl fresh cream
  • 2 dl Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 ml Pepper
  1. Boil the pasta according to the label
  2. Cut the bacon in 2cm pieces. Fry until crispy.
  3. Mix egg, cream, parmesan cheese, salt and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Remove the water from the pasta and put it back in the pot.
  5. Pour the eggmix and the bacon into the pot and mix it slowly on low heat until creamy.
