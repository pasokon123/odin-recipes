Lasagna

  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • 500g Minced beef
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 4 tbsp Tomato Puré
  • 1 tsp Dried thyme
  • 1 tsp Dried rosemary
  • 390g Crushed tomatoes
  • 1 Meat stock cube
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Heat the oven to 175
  2. Chop the onion and garlic. Fry together with the meat and add tomato puré. Season with thyme and rosemary.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and meat stock, let it boil for about 10 minutes.
  1. To make the sauce, melt the butter in a pot and pour in the wheat
  2. Keep adding milk while mixing
  3. Let the sauce boil for about 5 minutes, taste it and add salt and pepper.
  1. Make layers by adding sauce, lasagna squares and minced meat to a oven safe container
  2. Finish with sauce and cheese on top
  3. Cook in the oven for 40 minutes
