Schnitzel

  • 400g Pork
  • 2ml Salt
  • 1 ml Pepper
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1dl Wheat
  • 2dl Breadcrumbs
  • Butter or oils for frying
  1. Flatten the pork to four thin pieces and season with salt pepper
  2. Crack the eggs in a deep plate and whip them lightly
  3. Pour wheat and breadcrumbs in two other deep plates
  4. Dip the pork in the wheat on both sides, then in egg and lastly in the breadcrumbs
  5. Fry in plenty of butter or oil about 4 minutes on each side
