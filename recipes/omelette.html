Omelette

  • 6 Eggs
  • 1dl Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 tbsp butter
  1. Mix eggs, milk, salt and pepper lightly.
  2. Put the butter into a hot pan and wait until the butter starts to get some color
  3. Pour in the egg and milk mix and stir
  4. The omelette is finished when its creamy on the surface and has let go of the edges
