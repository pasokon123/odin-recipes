Fried Rice

  • 3dl Rice
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Red pepper
  • 150g Sweet peas
  • 2 Green onions
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Japanese soy sauce
  1. Cook the rice
  2. Peel the carrot and remove the seeds from the red pepper.
  3. Cut up the carrot, red pepper and sweet pees in slices.
  4. Fry the vegetables in a big frying pan for about 3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the rice to the frying pan and mix.
  6. Crack the eggs over the frying pan and mix until creamy.
